# AI Music Ad Hook Generator

## Problem
Creative fatigue limits paid growth performance in consumer AI apps.

## Hypothesis
LLMs can generate diverse, persona-specific ad hooks at scale, increasing testing velocity and reducing creative bottlenecks.

## Approach
- Input: Target persona
- Output: 20 categorised ad hooks
- Designed for rapid paid testing

## Why This Matters for AI Consumer Apps
AI-native marketing should:
- Automate ideation
- Increase iteration speed
- Personalise messaging by persona
